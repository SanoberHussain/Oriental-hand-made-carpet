For Oriental Carpet Category
Oriental carpets, mainly from countries across Asia and the Caucasus region, 
are handwoven or hand-knotted, made from the finest quality natural materials
 such as wool, silk, cotton, and sometimes animal hair.
Our selected ‘Oriental carpet designs’ typically feature intricate
 patterns and rich colors from the geographically "Rug Belt," stretching 
from Turkey through the Middle East and Central Asia.  These ‘Oriental carpet designs’ 
have a long history and cultural significance, woven by people of various cultures. 
Our carefully selected patterns of ‘Oriental’ carpets vary widely in size, design,
 materials, and weave techniques, and include items ranging from floor coverings to 
decorative pieces.
