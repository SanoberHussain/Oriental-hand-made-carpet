Tribal Rugs

Authentic Cultural Heritage

Our Tribal carpet designs are made by nomadic peoples, primarily from regions in Central Asia, the Caucasus, and Afghanistan, using designs that are spontaneous and deeply symbolic, reflecting the history, beliefs, and artistic traditions of the weaver's community.

Our selected Tribal rug patterns are predominantly geometric, featuring distinct symbols that carry deep cultural significance. These motifs have a more abstract, geometric, and stylized look, made from locally sourced sheep's wool, goat hair, and cotton, known for their durability and dyed with natural dyes made from plants and minerals, resulting in rich, earthy, and sometimes variegated colors. The color palette in our Tribal collection features warm, rich tones, especially deep reds, browns, rust, and blues, with accents of ivory, green, and yellow.
